Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum budget needed?

Google Profile management starts at ₹1,500/month. Paid ads management starts at ₹4,000/month plus your actual ad spend (we recommend a minimum of ₹5,000/month in ad spend to see results).

Do I need Google Ads or Meta Ads?

Google Ads works best when people actively search for your service (e.g. 'dentist near me'). Meta Ads work better for reaching people before they know they want something. We advise based on your specific business.

Do you work with businesses outside Maharashtra?

Yes. We work with businesses across India. Since our core services are digital, location is no barrier.

How long does it take to see results?

Paid Ads can generate leads within the first 48 hours. Google Business Profile optimization takes 4-8 weeks to naturally improve your local search ranking.
